Our Mission

Help people enjoy Ocean City while helping Ocean City thrive.

OCNJ.eth is an independent community project. It is not an official city website. The goal is to provide useful local information, support businesses, preserve memories, and create measurable community impact.

01

Local First

Highlight independent businesses and the people who work hard to serve Ocean City.

02

Useful Information

Create honest, practical guides instead of empty promotional content.

03

Preserve Memories

Collect photographs, family stories, traditions, and memories from former businesses.

04

Give Back

Promote nonprofits, cleanups, charitable projects, fundraisers, and volunteer opportunities.
